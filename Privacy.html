Privacy

------------------------------------------------------------
These are key privacy rules that website must abide to as to not get sue'd
----
Privacy by Design: Embed privacy into the development and operation of the website from the start.
----
Transparent Privacy Policy: Publicly display a clear, accessible policy detailing what data is collected, why, how it is used, and with who it is shared too.
----
Data Minimization: Only collect personal information that is absolutely necessary for your business functions.
----
Active User Consent: Obtain explicit consent for data collection, particularly for cookies, marketing, and third party tracking.
----
Data Retention Policies: Set automatic deletion policies for old or unnecessary data to minimize liability.
----
User Rights Management: Provide mechanisms for users to access, correct, delete (right to be forgotten), or object to the processing of their data.
----
Cookie Compliance: Implement a cookie consent banner, especially for GDPR compliance, allowing users to opt-out of non-essential tracking
----
