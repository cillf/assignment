Accessibility

------------------------------------------------------------
These 4 things must be appplied to maximise Accessibility, across your site:

Perceivable: Information must be presentable to users.
----
Operable: Components must be functional via keyboard not just mouse with it allowing enough time to read/use content.
----
Understandable: Content and operation must be clear and easy to see to help assist users so they avoid mistakes.
----
Robust: Content must work across various, including future, assistive technologies.
----
